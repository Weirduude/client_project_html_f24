Study Spaces on Campus

Introduction

Welcome to University of Michigan! This page provides an overview of several study locations on North and Central Campuses.

North Campus

Duderstadt Center (The Dude)
Duderstadt Center (The Dude)

A versatile study space with computer labs, collaborative rooms, and quiet areas. It also has the Engineering Library and the famous Media Union.

Pierpont Commons
Pierpont Commons

Various seating areas and study spaces are available, including the Atrium and the Fireside Cafe.

North Campus Research Complex (NCRC)
North Campus Research Complex (NCRC)

Some buildings have specific study areas available for students.


Central Campus

Undergraduate Library (UGLi)
Undergraduate Library (UGLi):

The UGLi is a popular study spot for undergraduate students. It has a mix of individual and group study spaces, as well as computer labs.

Hatcher Graduate Library
Hatcher Graduate Library

The Hatcher Graduate Library is a popular study spot for graduate students. It has a mix of individual and group study spaces, as well as computer labs.

Michigan Union
Michigan Union

The Michigan Union has various study spaces, including lounges, cafes, and the Willis Ward Lounge.
