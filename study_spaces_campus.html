Study Spaces on Campus

Skip to Main Content

North Campus

  • Duderstadt Center (The Dude)

    A versatile study space with computer labs, collaborative rooms, and quiet areas. It also has the Engineering Library and the famous Media Union.

  • Pierpont Commons

    Various seating areas and study spaces are available, including the Atrium and the Fireside Cafe.

  • North Campus Research Complex (NCRC)

    Some buildings have specific study areas available for students.

Central Campus

  • Undergraduate Library (UGLi):
    • First Floor: More social and group study-friendly.
    • Second and Third Floors: Quieter with more individual study spaces.
    • Computer Labs: Available for students who need computer access.
  • Hatcher Graduate Library
    • Reference Reading Room: A classic, quiet study spot.
    • Gallery: Good for collaborative work.
    • Graduate Study Room: Reserved for grad students, very quiet.
